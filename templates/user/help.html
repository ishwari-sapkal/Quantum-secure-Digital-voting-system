How do I cast my vote? +
Log in with your Voter ID and select your candidate on the voting page. Once submitted, your vote cannot be changed.
How can I view my voter card? +
Go to your profile and click on "View Voter Card". You can print or save it as PDF for your records.
What if I forget my Voter ID? +
Contact the election office or apply for a new voter card using the "Apply for Voter ID" option in your profile.
How can I check election results? +
After the election, results will be posted in the "Notice" section of your dashboard.
How do I report issues or errors? +
Use the feedback or complaint section in your profile to report any issues. The admin team will review your submission.
